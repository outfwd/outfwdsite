About Us

Built by drivers, run for shippers

Outforward Logistics has grown from a single truck to a nationwide fleet — without losing the personal service that got us here.

Our Story

Trucking done right

Outforward Logistics was founded by people who spent years behind the wheel and knew the industry needed carriers that actually keep their word. Today we run dry van and refrigerated freight across the country, backed by a dispatch team that treats every load — and every driver — with respect.

Whether you're a shipper who needs freight moved reliably or an owner-operator looking for a real partner, we built this company for you.

At a glance

  • Founded to fix what's broken in trucking
  • Dry van and reefer capacity nationwide
  • Driver-first culture and fair pay
  • Real people answering the phone, not a call center
